# Sports2000 Database Schema Summary

This is a summary of the sports2000 database schema. The full schema is available in sports2000.df.

## Sequences

- NextCustNum: Customer numbers (starts at 1000, increment 5)
- NextOrdNum: Order numbers (starts at 1000, increment 5)
- NextItemNum: Item numbers (starts at 100, increment 10)
- NextInvNum: Invoice numbers (starts at 1000, increment 1)
- NextRefNum: Reference numbers (starts at 1, increment 1)
- NextVisitor: Visitor numbers (starts at 1000, increment 1)
- NextSupplNum: Supplier numbers (starts at 0, increment 1)
- NextEmpNum: Employee numbers (starts at 0, increment 1)

## Main Tables

### Customer
Customer information including contact details, credit limits, and sales representatives.

### Order
Customer orders with order dates, ship dates, and carrier information.

### OrderLine
Line items for orders, linking to items with quantities and prices.

### Item
Product catalog with item numbers, names, prices, and inventory levels.

### Benefits
Employee benefits information.

### Employee
Employee records with personal and employment information.

### Warehouse
Warehouse locations and information.

### Supplier
Supplier information for inventory management.

For complete schema details including all fields, indexes, and relationships, refer to dump/sports2000.df in the source repository.
